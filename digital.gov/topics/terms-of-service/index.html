Terms of service

Agreements negotiated between the federal government and vendors who offer digital tools and services.

Social media is a key way that federal agencies engage with their customers and accomplish their mission. However, there are risks and legal implications associated with these tools. Be sure to coordinate with your Office of General Counsel before signing any terms of service or end-user license agreements.

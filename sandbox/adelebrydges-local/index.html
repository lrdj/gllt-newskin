Pleasure is a Journey

Adele Brydges is a London-based ceramist and sculptor exploring sensuality, connection and embodiment through functional and sculptural works and experiences. Her practice invites self-exploration and challenges perceptions of intimacy, encouraging deeper relationships with self and other, while highlighting the transformative power of pleasure.

At the core of Adeles work is a desire to reimagine societal narratives around the erotic and foster meaningful conversations on sensual awareness. She views the body as a home we inhabit and nourish through pleasure, touch, and interconnection. Each piece is a vessel for communion, channeling her works into symbols of emotional resonance and healing.

Through her work, she creates spaces for reflection and dialogue, offering new ways to experience intimacy, self-acceptance, and synergy. Learn more

Pleasure tools: Philsophy, Craftsmanship & Pleasure

  • There was a young woman with a deep desire to experience a profound sense of connection, and use pleasure as a tool for self acceptance and love. The tools available to help her on this quest just weren't "it" though.

    For someone who was a Bachelor of Arts, and a graduate of ceramic design, the rampant rabits and disco dicks on the high street were so unappealing. So, in 2006, driven by a passion to craft conscious, beautiful, and engaging tools that were evocative, tactile, aesthetic, and functional, she began to create porcelain pleasure tools .

  • Adele believes that pleasure is a journey, not a destination, and her creations embody this philosophy. Sensuality, connection and beauty are the foundational values that guide her work and process.

    Handcrafting each piece from porcelain in her London studio, Adele dedicates over two weeks to create every piece. Her meticulous process and Nerikomi-inspired technique ensure that no two pieces are alike, making each one a unique work of sensual art. This technique involves layering and blending different colors of porcelain, resulting in intricate patterns and textures that enhance both the visual and tactile experience.

  • More than just functional items, Adele's pleasure tools are intimate works of art designed to deepen one's connection with self and other. Her creations invite users to explore their sensuality and embrace the beauty of the present moment. By focusing on the journey of pleasure, Adele transforms everyday experiences into moments of profound connection and self-discovery.

Sensual alchemy is the art of transforming and exploring sensual experiences, emotions, and perceptions through creative, energetic, and relational practices.

It offers a juicy, rich, and poetic way of living, treating life as art and deepening our experiences of pleasure, intimacy, and self-awareness.

We all have the potential to be sensual alchemists. This journey encompasses personal growth, self-discovery, and the integration of mind, body, and spirit.

Guided by her creative process, esoteric studies, and personal experiences, Adele creates tools to elevate and enhance self-pleasure and prescence practices. She hosts creative experiences to facilitate authentic expression and creative connection and holds space for energetic awareness and emotional release in her energy practice.
